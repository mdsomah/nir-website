Hon. Marcus G. W. Pyne

Deputy Executive Director for Administration
National Identification Registry(NIR)

Hon Marcus G. W. Pyne earned his Bachelor of Business Administration-Business Management in 2010 and also Bachelor of Business Administration in Accounting 1998 at the University of the District of Columbia respectively, Washington, D.C., USA.

After his graduation from High School, he enrolled at the Modern Commercial Institute, Center Street, Monrovia, where he earned his first Associate Degree in Accounting 1984.

During his tenure at Vivian Management Team/JB Johnson Nursing Center as Staff Accountant, he performed the following duties: payroll processing of 550-600 employees bi-weekly payroll full circle; researching and reclassifying accounts in the general ledger; processing direct deposits; and child support and garnishment. He was also responsible for the processing of electronic payment of Federal Income Taxes 940 and 941 and monthly payment of State Taxes.

Hon. Pyne worked at Jackson Hewitt as Tax Consultant, preparing individual and business taxes.While working at Jackson Hewitt, other duties included: mortgage interest, real estate taxes, property taxes, mutual funds, bonds and stocks.

While in Liberia, he worked with the F&F Electronic Inc. as Chief of Staff/Administrative Assistant to the General Manager. Duties included: supervision of the administrative and technical staff of the various departments, monitoring the shipping and import of Wang Computers and Motorola Handsets plus Harris Corporation Radio Communication Installation for Liberia Rural Broadcasting Communication Network in the various counties during the eighties. The handling of monthly payroll and invoices for vendors, bank statements reconciliation, petty cash expenses and deposit to the general ledger.

Skill applied; Wang Processor and Lotus 1-2-3.
